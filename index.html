Who I am

The Story behind CraftSupport

Craftsupport was made because I like to help and get my hands dirty with all sorts of problems, so I thought, why not make it into a business? It's always been a dream of mine to make something I love into something I can do for fun and earn a bit of money on the side while helping people with problems they can't figure out themselves.

I offer Plugin Configuration, Bug Fixing, Error Debugging, and more. While I have many years of on/off experience, I don't know how to code plugins from scratch, but if necessary, I will figure something out. My goal is to provide cheap and hassle-free support so you can get on with what matters, having fun and playing on your server.
